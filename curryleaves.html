🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Curry Leaves / Kadi Patta

Botanical Name: Murraya koenigii

Family: Rutaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Curry leaves help in improving digestion and reducing acidity.

They are beneficial for controlling blood sugar levels and managing diabetes.

They promote hair growth and prevent premature greying.

🌍 Habitat

Curry leaf plants grow well in tropical and subtropical climates with moderate sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

They are grown from seeds or stem cuttings and require well-drained soil and regular watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Generally safe, but excessive consumption may cause mild stomach discomfort.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Carbazole Alkaloids: Antioxidant
Vitamin A & C: Boost immunity
Flavonoids: Anti-inflammatory
