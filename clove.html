🌿 Clove (Laung)

The Natural Pain Reliever

Home Plants About Contact

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Clove / Laung

Botanical Name: Syzygium aromaticum

Family: Myrtaceae

Origin: Indonesia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Clove is widely known for its strong antiseptic and pain-relieving properties.

It is commonly used to relieve toothache and improve oral health.

Clove also aids digestion, boosts immunity, and has antibacterial effects.

🌍 Habitat

Clove trees grow in tropical climates with high humidity and rainfall.

🌾 Cultivation

It is cultivated from seeds and requires rich, well-drained soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use may cause irritation or allergic reactions in some individuals.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Eugenol: Pain reliever
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
Tannins: Antimicrobial
