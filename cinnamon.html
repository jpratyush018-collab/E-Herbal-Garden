🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Cinnamon / Dalchini

Botanical Name: Cinnamomum verum

Family: Lauraceae

Origin: Sri Lanka

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Helps regulate blood sugar levels.

Improves digestion and metabolism.

Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in tropical climates with high humidity.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown from cuttings in fertile, well-drained soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may affect liver due to coumarin.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Cinnamaldehyde: Antimicrobial
Coumarin: Blood thinner
Polyphenols: Antioxidant
