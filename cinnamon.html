🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Cinnamon / Dalchini

Botanical Name: Cinnamomum verum

Family: Lauraceae

Origin: Sri Lanka

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Cinnamon is known for its ability to regulate blood sugar levels.

It helps improve metabolism, digestion, and heart health.

It also has strong antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

Cinnamon grows in tropical climates with warm temperatures and high humidity.

🌾 Cultivation

It is cultivated from cuttings and requires fertile, well-drained soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause liver issues due to coumarin content.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Cinnamaldehyde: Antimicrobial
Coumarin: Blood thinner
Polyphenols: Antioxidant
