🧬 Overview

Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum and Cinnamomum cassia) is a widely used medicinal and aromatic plant known for its therapeutic, antimicrobial, and metabolic health benefits. It has been extensively studied for its role in blood sugar regulation, antioxidant activity, and infection control.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Common Name: Cinnamon

Botanical Name: Cinnamomum verum / Cinnamomum cassia

Family: Lauraceae

Plant Type: Evergreen tree

Habitat: Tropical regions
