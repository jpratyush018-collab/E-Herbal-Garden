🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Neem

Botanical Name: Azadirachta indica

Family: Meliaceae

Origin: Indian Subcontinent

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Neem is known for strong antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties. It helps treat skin diseases like acne, infections, and wounds.

It purifies blood, boosts immunity, and is used in oral care for healthy gums and teeth.

Neem oil is useful for hair care, dandruff removal, and scalp infections.

🌍 Habitat

Neem grows in tropical and semi-tropical regions with temperatures between 25°C to 40°C.

It needs full sunlight and grows well even in dry and poor soil conditions.

🌾 Cultivation

Neem is grown from seeds and requires minimal care once established.

It is drought-resistant and grows well in sandy or rocky soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake of neem can be harmful, especially for children.

Neem oil should not be consumed directly.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Azadirachtin: Insecticidal
Nimbin: Anti-inflammatory
Nimbidin: Antibacterial
Quercetin: Antioxidant
