🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Neem

Botanical Name: Azadirachta indica

Family: Meliaceae

Origin: Indian Subcontinent

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Neem is known for strong antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties.

It helps treat skin diseases like acne, infections, and wounds.

It purifies blood, boosts immunity, and improves oral health.

🌍 Habitat

Neem grows in tropical and semi-tropical regions with temperatures between 25°C to 40°C.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds and requires minimal care once established.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may be harmful, especially for children.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Azadirachtin: Insecticidal
Nimbin: Anti-inflammatory
Nimbidin: Antibacterial
Quercetin: Antioxidant
