🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Thyme

Botanical Name: Thymus vulgaris

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Thyme is known for its strong antibacterial and antifungal properties.

It helps in treating cough, cold, and respiratory issues.

It also supports digestion and boosts immunity.

🌍 Habitat

It grows in dry, sunny climates with well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

Thyme is grown from seeds or cuttings and requires minimal watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use of thyme oil may cause irritation; use in moderation.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Thymol: Antiseptic
Carvacrol: Antibacterial
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
