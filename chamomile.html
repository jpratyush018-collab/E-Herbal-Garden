🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Chamomile

Botanical Name: Matricaria chamomilla

Chamomile is a well-known medicinal herb famous for its calming properties and is widely used in herbal teas, skincare, and traditional medicine.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

😌 Relaxation & Stress Relief: Helps calm the mind and reduce anxiety.

😴 Sleep Aid: Promotes better sleep and helps with insomnia.

🦠 Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and soothes irritation.

🍵 Digestive Health: Helps relieve indigestion, bloating, and stomach cramps.

💆 Skin Care: Used for soothing skin, reducing redness, and healing wounds.

🌍 Habitat

Chamomile is native to Europe and Western Asia but is now cultivated worldwide. It grows well in temperate climates and open sunny areas.

🌾 Cultivation

Sunlight: Prefers full sunlight

Soil: Well-drained, moderately fertile soil

Watering: Moderate watering required

Propagation: Grown from seeds

Chamomile is easy to grow and is commonly cultivated in gardens and farms.

⚠️ Precautions

Generally safe when consumed in moderate amounts.

May cause allergic reactions in individuals sensitive to daisy-family plants.

Excessive use may lead to nausea or dizziness.

Consult a healthcare professional if pregnant or under medication.
