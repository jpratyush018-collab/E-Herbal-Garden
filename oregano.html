🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Oregano

Botanical Name: Origanum vulgare

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Oregano has strong antibacterial and antifungal properties.

It helps in boosting immunity and fighting infections.

It also supports digestion and respiratory health.

🌍 Habitat

It grows in warm, dry climates with well-drained soil and sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Oregano is grown from seeds or cuttings and requires minimal watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use of oregano oil may cause irritation; use in moderation.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Carvacrol: Antibacterial
Thymol: Antiseptic
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
