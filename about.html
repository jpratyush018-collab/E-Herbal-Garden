🌿 About E-Herbal Garden 🌿

Welcome to E-Herbal Garden

E-Herbal Garden is a digital platform designed to spread awareness about medicinal plants and Ayurveda. It combines traditional knowledge with modern technology to create an interactive and engaging learning experience.

Our platform allows users to explore different plants, understand their uses, and learn about the importance of Ayurveda in daily life. We aim to make ancient knowledge simple, accessible, and useful for everyone.

🌱 Our Mission

To promote awareness about medicinal plants and natural healing methods.

💡 What We Offer

Interactive plant data, Ayurvedic knowledge, and upcoming 3D visualization features.

🚀 Future Vision

To integrate AI chatbot, smart search, and advanced 3D plant models.
