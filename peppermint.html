🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Peppermint

Botanical Name: Mentha × piperita

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Europe and Middle East

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Peppermint is widely used to relieve digestive issues like indigestion and bloating.

It helps in reducing headaches and provides a cooling effect.

It also supports respiratory health and relieves cold symptoms.

🌍 Habitat

Peppermint grows in moist, shaded areas with rich soil.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from cuttings and requires regular watering and partial sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use may cause heartburn or irritation in sensitive individuals.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Menthol: Cooling and soothing
Menthone: Digestive aid
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
