🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Mustard

Botanical Name: Brassica juncea

Family: Brassicaceae

Origin: Asia and Europe

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Mustard seeds are used to improve digestion and stimulate appetite.

They help in relieving muscle pain and joint aches when applied as oil.

Mustard also has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

Mustard grows in cool climates with well-drained fertile soil.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds and requires moderate watering and sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use may cause irritation or allergic reactions in some people.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Glucosinolates: Antioxidant
Allyl Isothiocyanate: Antimicrobial
Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Heart health
