🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Onion / Pyaz

Botanical Name: Allium cepa

Family: Amaryllidaceae

Origin: Central Asia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Onion helps in boosting immunity and fighting infections.

It supports heart health and helps regulate blood sugar levels.

It also has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

🌍 Habitat

It grows in temperate climates with well-drained soil and sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Onions are grown from seeds or bulbs and require regular watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause acidity or stomach discomfort.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Quercetin: Antioxidant
Sulfur Compounds: Heart health
Flavonoids: Anti-inflammatory
