🌿 Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

The Vitamin C Powerhouse

Home Plants About Contact

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Amla / Indian Gooseberry

Botanical Name: Phyllanthus emblica

Family: Phyllanthaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Amla is one of the richest sources of Vitamin C and is widely used to boost immunity.

It helps in improving digestion, enhancing metabolism, and detoxifying the body.

Amla is also beneficial for hair growth, skin health, and improving eyesight.

🌍 Habitat

Amla grows in tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm climates.

🌾 Cultivation

Amla is grown from seeds or grafting and requires well-drained soil and sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause acidity or dryness in some individuals.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Vitamin C: Immunity booster
Tannins: Antioxidant
Gallic Acid: Anti-inflammatory
