🧬 Overview

Ginger is a medicinal plant known for its digestive, anti-inflammatory, and immune-supporting properties.

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Ginger

Botanical Name: Zingiber officinale

Family: Zingiberaceae

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Supports digestion, reduces nausea, improves immunity, and relieves inflammation.

🌍 Habitat

Tropical and subtropical regions.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown through rhizomes in warm climates.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause mild irritation.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Plant Type: Perennial herb

Habitat: Tropical regions

Parts Used: Rhizome

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Rhizome Division: Primary method

🌞 Growth Requirements

  • Warm climate
  • Fertile soil
  • Partial shade
  • Regular watering

🩺 Therapeutic Applications

Widely studied for digestion, inflammation, and metabolic health.
