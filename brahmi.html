🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Brahmi

Botanical Name: Bacopa monnieri

Family: Plantaginaceae

Origin: India and Southeast Asia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Brahmi is widely known for improving memory, concentration, and brain function. It is commonly used in Ayurveda as a brain tonic.

It helps reduce stress, anxiety, and improves mental clarity.

Brahmi also supports nervous system health and enhances learning ability.

🌍 Habitat

Brahmi grows in wet and marshy areas. It prefers warm climates and moist soil.

🌾 Cultivation

Brahmi is grown from cuttings and spreads easily in moist environments.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause nausea or stomach discomfort.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Bacosides: Memory enhancement
Alkaloids: Nervous system support
Saponins: Antioxidant
