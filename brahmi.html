🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Brahmi

Botanical Name: Bacopa monnieri

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Brahmi is widely used to enhance memory, improve concentration, reduce stress, and support overall brain health.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in wetlands, marshy areas, and near water bodies.

🌾 Cultivation

Primarily grown through stem cuttings in moist or waterlogged soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause mild digestive discomfort. Use with guidance.

🧬 Overview

Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri) is a medicinal herb known for its cognitive-enhancing and neuroprotective properties. It supports memory, learning, and mental clarity.

The leaves and stems are mainly used for medicinal purposes.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Family: Plantaginaceae

Plant Type: Creeping perennial herb

Habitat: Wetlands and marshy areas

Parts Used: Leaves and stems

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Stem Cuttings: Small segments grow quickly in moist soil.

Seeds: Less commonly used.

🌞 Growth Requirements

Moist or waterlogged soil

Partial to full sunlight

Regular watering
