🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Sage

Botanical Name: Salvia officinalis

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Sage is known for improving memory and brain function.

It helps in relieving sore throat and digestive problems.

It also has strong antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

Sage grows in dry, sunny climates with well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds or cuttings and requires moderate watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause dizziness; avoid overuse.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Thujone: Antimicrobial
Cineole: Respiratory support
Rosmarinic Acid: Anti-inflammatory
