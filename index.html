History of Ayurveda 🌿

Ayurveda, meaning "Science of Life", is one of the oldest healing systems in the world, originating over 5000 years ago in India.

It focuses on balance between body, mind, and spirit using natural remedies, herbs, diet, and lifestyle practices.

  • Vata – Movement & nervous system
  • Pitta – Digestion & metabolism
  • Kapha – Stability & structure
1500 BCE: Origin in Vedas
1000 BCE: Oral teachings by sages
500 BCE: Charaka & Sushruta contributions
Medieval: Spread across Asia
Colonial: Decline phase
Modern: Global revival

Today Ayurveda is widely used for immunity, stress relief, and wellness worldwide.
