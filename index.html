📜 History of Ayurveda

🌌 Vedic Period (5000+ years ago)

Ayurveda originated in the Atharva Veda. Ancient sages observed nature and documented healing herbs, diseases, and spiritual therapies.

📚 Samhita Era (1000 BCE)

Knowledge compiled in great texts: Charaka Samhita, Sushruta Samhita, Ashtanga Hridaya.

🏥 Golden Age (600 BCE - 200 CE)

Sushruta performed world's first plastic surgery. Over 300 procedures and 120 instruments developed.

🌍 Global Spread (200 BCE onwards)

Traveled via Silk Road to China, Tibet, Greece. Influenced Unani, TCM. Arab scholars translated texts.

✨ Modern Revival (20th Century)

India's Ministry of AYUSH promotes globally. Now practiced in 100+ countries as complementary medicine.

Vedic Origin

Ayurveda is believed to have originated during the Atharva Veda. Healing herbs 🌱, diseases 🦠, spiritual therapies 🧘.

Ancient Texts

Knowledge of Ayurveda was systematically written in Samhitas: Charaka Samhita, Sushruta Samhita, Ashtanga Hridaya.

Sushruta

Pioneer of surgery. 300+ surgical procedures, 120 instruments, early plastic surgery, author of Sushruta Samhita.

Branches

8 branches (Ashtanga Ayurveda): Kayachikitsa, Shalya Tantra, Shalakya Tantra, Kaumarbhritya, Agada Tantra, Bhuta Vidya, Rasayana, Vajikarana.

Global Spread

Influenced China, Tibet, Middle East. Translated by Arab scholars. Practiced in 100+ countries.

Modern Use

Promoted by Ministry of AYUSH. Immunity boost, Yoga & meditation, Panchakarma detox, Herbal medicines.
