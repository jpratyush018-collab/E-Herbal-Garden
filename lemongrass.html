🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Lemongrass

Botanical Name: Cymbopogon citratus

Family: Poaceae

Origin: Southeast Asia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Lemongrass is widely used for its calming and digestive properties.

It helps reduce anxiety, improve sleep, and relieve stomach problems like bloating and indigestion.

It also has antibacterial and antifungal properties and is used in herbal teas and oils.

🌍 Habitat

Lemongrass grows well in tropical and subtropical climates with plenty of sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from stalks and requires well-drained soil and regular watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause dizziness or allergic reactions in some people.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Citral: Antimicrobial
Limonene: Antioxidant
Geraniol: Anti-inflammatory
