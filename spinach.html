🌿 Spinach (Palak)

The Leafy Nutrient Powerhouse

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Spinach / Palak

Botanical Name: Spinacia oleracea

Family: Amaranthaceae

Origin: Central Asia

🧬 Overview

Spinach is a nutrient-rich leafy vegetable packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

✅ Rich in iron and vitamins

✅ Supports heart health

✅ Anti-inflammatory & antioxidant

✅ Boosts immunity

🌍 Habitat

Thrives in cool climates with fertile, well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown from seeds, needs moderate watering and sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause kidney stone issues due to oxalates.

🧪 Compounds

Vitamins A, C, K, Iron, Magnesium, Flavonoids

💊 Applications

Supports blood health, immunity, and digestion.
