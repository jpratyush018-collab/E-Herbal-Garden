🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Basil / Tulsi

Botanical Name: Ocimum basilicum (Sweet Basil) / Ocimum sanctum (Tulsi)

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: India and Tropical Asia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Basil is widely used to boost immunity and fight infections.

It helps in relieving cough, cold, and respiratory disorders.

It also reduces stress and improves digestion.

🌍 Habitat

Basil grows in warm climates with plenty of sunlight and well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

It is easily grown from seeds and requires regular watering and sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause mild side effects; use in moderation.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Eugenol: Anti-inflammatory
Ursolic Acid: Antioxidant
Rosmarinic Acid: Antimicrobial
