🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Parsley

Botanical Name: Petroselinum crispum

Family: Apiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean region

Plant Type: Biennial herb

Parts Used: Leaves, seeds, roots

🌿 Medicinal Uses

✅ Rich in antioxidants and vitamins (A, C, K)

✅ Supports kidney and liver health

✅ Acts as a natural diuretic

✅ Boosts immunity and digestion

🌍 Habitat

Thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown from seeds, needs sunlight and regular watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Avoid excessive consumption, especially during pregnancy.
