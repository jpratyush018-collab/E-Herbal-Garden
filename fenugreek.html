🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Fenugreek / Methi

Botanical Name: Trigonella foenum-graecum

Family: Fabaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region & India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Fenugreek is widely used for controlling blood sugar levels and managing diabetes.

It helps improve digestion, reduce cholesterol, and boost metabolism.

It is also beneficial for hair growth and improving skin health.

🌍 Habitat

Fenugreek grows in warm climates and is commonly cultivated in India.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds and requires well-drained soil and moderate watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause stomach upset or allergic reactions.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Diosgenin: Hormonal balance
Alkaloids: Medicinal effects
Fiber: Digestive health
