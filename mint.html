🌿 Peppermint (Mentha × piperita)

The Cooling Medicinal Herb

Home Plants About Contact

🧬 Overview

Peppermint is a widely used medicinal herb known for its cooling sensation and therapeutic benefits. It supports digestion, respiratory health, and relaxation, and is extensively used in herbal medicine.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Family: Lamiaceae

Common Name: Peppermint

Botanical Name: Mentha × piperita

Plant Type: Perennial herb

Habitat: Temperate regions

Parts Used: Leaves

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Cuttings: Most effective method

Runners: Natural spreading method

🌞 Growth Requirements

  • Moist, well-drained soil
  • Partial to full sunlight
  • Regular watering
  • Cool to moderate climate

🧪 Key Bioactive Compounds

  • Menthol
  • Menthone
  • Flavonoids
  • Essential oils
  • Rosmarinic acid

💊 Pharmacological Properties

  • Digestive support
  • Respiratory relief
  • Stress & headache relief
  • Antimicrobial
  • Antioxidant

🩺 Therapeutic Applications

  • Digestive health
  • Respiratory health
  • Pain relief
  • Infection control
  • General wellness

⚙️ Mechanism of Action

  • Menthol provides cooling effect
  • Relaxes digestive muscles
  • Fights microbes
  • Reduces oxidative stress

⚠️ Safety & Precautions

  • Safe in moderate amounts
  • Excess may cause heartburn
  • Avoid overuse of concentrated oils

🌍 Modern Relevance

Peppermint is recognized in modern herbal medicine for digestive health, antimicrobial effects, and IBS management.
