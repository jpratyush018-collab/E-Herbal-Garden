🌿 Mint (Pudina)

The Cooling Herb

💊 Pharmacological & Medicinal Properties

🍽️ Digestive Aid: Relieves indigestion, bloating, and gas

🦠 Antimicrobial: Fights bacteria, fungi, and viruses

❄️ Cooling & Soothing: Reduces irritation and inflammation

🔥 Anti-inflammatory: Reduces pain and inflammation in muscles and skin

🛡️ Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress

🩺 Therapeutic Applications

Digestive Health: Helps in IBS, indigestion, and nausea

Respiratory Support: Relieves congestion, cough, and asthma symptoms

Oral Care: Used in toothpaste and mouthwash for antimicrobial benefits

Skin Care: Reduces inflammation, irritation, and acne

Mental Health: Refreshing aroma helps reduce stress and headaches

⚙️ Mechanism of Action

Menthol activates cold receptors in the skin and digestive tract

Flavonoids and essential oils inhibit microbial growth

Anti-inflammatory compounds reduce cytokine release and oxidative stress

🌍 Relevance in Modern Healthcare

Mint is widely used in pharmaceuticals, food flavoring, cosmetics, and herbal remedies.

Recognized by NCBI, WHO, and Ministry of AYUSH for its digestive, respiratory, and antimicrobial properties.
