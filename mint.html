🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Peppermint

Botanical Name: Mentha × piperita

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Peppermint helps relieve digestive issues, headaches, and respiratory problems.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in temperate regions.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown through cuttings and runners in moist soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess use may cause heartburn or discomfort.

🧬 Overview

Peppermint (Mentha × piperita) is a medicinal herb known for its cooling sensation and therapeutic properties. It supports digestion, respiratory health, and relaxation.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Family: Lamiaceae

Plant Type: Perennial herb

Habitat: Temperate regions

Parts Used: Leaves

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Cuttings: Most effective method

Runners: Natural spreading method

🌞 Growth Requirements

  • Moist, well-drained soil
  • Partial to full sunlight
  • Regular watering
  • Cool to moderate climate

🧪 Key Bioactive Compounds

  • Menthol
  • Menthone
  • Flavonoids
  • Essential oils
  • Rosmarinic acid

💊 Pharmacological Properties

  • Digestive support
  • Respiratory relief
  • Stress & headache relief
  • Antimicrobial
  • Antioxidant

🩺 Therapeutic Applications

  • Digestive health
  • Respiratory health
  • Pain & headache relief
  • Infection control
  • General wellness

🦠 Public Health Importance

Widely studied for digestive health, antimicrobial effects, and IBS treatment.

⚙️ Mechanism of Action

  • Menthol provides cooling effect
  • Relaxes digestive muscles
  • Fights microbes
  • Reduces oxidative stress

⚠️ Safety

Safe in moderate amounts. Excess may cause discomfort.

🌍 Modern Relevance

Recognized by NCBI, WHO, and Ministry of AYUSH for therapeutic benefits.
