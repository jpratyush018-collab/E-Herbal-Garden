🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Parsley

Botanical Name: Petroselinum crispum

Family: Apiaceae

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Rich in vitamins A, C, K. Supports digestion, immunity, and kidney health.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

Needs sunlight, regular watering, and fertile soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Consume in moderation. Avoid excess during pregnancy.
