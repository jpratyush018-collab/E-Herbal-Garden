🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Eucalyptus

Botanical Name: Eucalyptus globulus

Family: Myrtaceae

Origin: Australia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Eucalyptus is widely used for respiratory relief and cold treatment.

It helps in clearing nasal congestion and improving breathing.

It also has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

It grows in warm climates with well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds or cuttings and requires moderate watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Eucalyptus oil should not be consumed directly without medical advice.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Eucalyptol: Respiratory support
Tannins: Antibacterial
Flavonoids: Anti-inflammatory
