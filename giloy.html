🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Giloy / Guduchi

Botanical Name: Tinospora cordifolia

Family: Menispermaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Giloy is a powerful immunity booster and is widely used in Ayurveda for treating various diseases.

It helps in reducing fever, especially in dengue and malaria.

Giloy also improves digestion, detoxifies the body, and enhances overall health.

🌍 Habitat

Giloy grows in tropical regions and is commonly found climbing on trees.

🌾 Cultivation

Giloy is grown from stem cuttings and requires support to climb.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause low blood sugar levels.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Tinosporin: Immunity booster
Alkaloids: Anti-inflammatory
Glycosides: Health support
