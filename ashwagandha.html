🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Ashwagandha / Indian Ginseng

Botanical Name: Withania somnifera

Family: Solanaceae

Origin: India, Middle East, and parts of Africa

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Ashwagandha is a powerful adaptogen that helps the body manage stress and anxiety. It is widely used to improve mental health and reduce cortisol levels.

It enhances strength, stamina, and energy levels, making it popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

Ashwagandha also supports brain function, improves memory, and boosts immunity.

🌍 Habitat

Ashwagandha grows in dry regions and prefers subtropical climates. It thrives in temperatures between 20°C to 35°C.

The plant grows well in sandy, well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Ashwagandha is grown from seeds and requires low maintenance.

It needs minimal watering and grows best in dry soil conditions.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause stomach upset or drowsiness.

Pregnant women and people with medical conditions should consult a doctor before use.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Withanolides: Anti-stress and anti-inflammatory
Alkaloids: Nervous system support
Saponins: Immunity boosting
Flavonoids: Antioxidant properties
