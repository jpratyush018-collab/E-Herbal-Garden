🌿 Rosemary (Rosmarinus officinalis)

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Rosemary

Botanical Name: Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosemary is a fragrant evergreen herb native to the Mediterranean region, widely known for its culinary, medicinal, and aromatic uses.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

🧠 Memory Enhancement: Improves concentration and brain function.

💆 Stress Relief: Helps reduce stress and mental fatigue.

🦠 Antimicrobial: Fights bacteria and infections.

🔥 Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and pain.

🫀 Circulation Support: Improves blood circulation.

🌍 Habitat

Rosemary grows naturally in warm, dry climates, especially in Mediterranean regions. It thrives in sunny environments with well-drained soil and is commonly cultivated in gardens worldwide.

🌾 Cultivation

Sunlight: Requires full sunlight

Soil: Well-drained, sandy or loamy soil

Watering: Low to moderate (avoid overwatering)

Propagation: Grown from seeds or stem cuttings

It is a hardy plant that requires minimal maintenance once established.

⚠️ Precautions

Generally safe when used in moderate amounts.

Excessive use may cause digestive discomfort or allergic reactions.

Pregnant women and individuals with medical conditions should consult a healthcare professional before use.
