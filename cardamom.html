🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Cardamom / Elaichi

Botanical Name: Elettaria cardamomum

Family: Zingiberaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Cardamom is widely used to improve digestion and relieve acidity.

It helps in freshening breath and maintaining oral health.

It also supports respiratory health and acts as a natural detoxifier.

🌍 Habitat

Cardamom grows in tropical forests with high humidity and shade.

🌾 Cultivation

It is cultivated from seeds and requires rich soil, shade, and regular watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause allergic reactions in rare cases.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Cineole: Respiratory support
Terpenes: Antioxidant
Flavonoids: Anti-inflammatory
