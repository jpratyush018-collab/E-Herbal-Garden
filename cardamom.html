🧬 Overview

Cardamom (Elettaria cardamomum) is a highly valued medicinal and aromatic plant known for its digestive, antimicrobial, and therapeutic properties.

🌱 Botanical Information

Common Name: Cardamom

Botanical Name: Elettaria cardamomum

Family: Zingiberaceae

🌿 Medicinal Uses

  • Improves digestion and relieves bloating
  • Supports respiratory health
  • Acts as natural antimicrobial
  • Rich in antioxidants

🌾 Cultivation

  • Grows in warm, humid climates
  • Needs fertile, well-drained soil
  • Requires partial shade and watering

⚠️ Precautions

  • Safe in moderate amounts
  • Avoid excessive consumption
