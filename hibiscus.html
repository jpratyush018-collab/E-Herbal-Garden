🧬 Overview

Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis and Hibiscus sabdariffa) is a widely recognized medicinal and ornamental plant known for its antioxidant, cardiovascular, and therapeutic properties. It supports heart health and overall wellness.

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Hibiscus

Botanical Name: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis / Hibiscus sabdariffa

Family: Malvaceae

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Used for heart health, skin care, digestion, and immunity support.

🌍 Habitat

Tropical and subtropical regions.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown using stem cuttings and seeds.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause mild side effects.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Plant Type: Shrub or small tree

Parts Used: Flowers, leaves, calyces

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Stem Cuttings: Most effective method

Seeds: Also used but slower

🌞 Growth Requirements

Well-drained soil

Warm climate

Full sunlight to partial shade

Regular watering

🧪 Key Bioactive Compounds

Anthocyanins, Flavonoids, Organic acids, Polyphenols, Vitamin C

💊 Pharmacological Properties

Cardiovascular support, antioxidant, metabolic support, anti-inflammatory

🩺 Therapeutic Applications

Heart health, blood pressure management, herbal tea, wellness

🌍 Modern Relevance

Recognized by NCBI, WHO, and Ministry of AYUSH for health benefits.
