🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Hibiscus / Gudhal

Botanical Name: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

Family: Malvaceae

Origin: East Asia

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Hibiscus is widely used for its health and beauty benefits. It helps in lowering blood pressure and improving heart health.

It is commonly used in hair care to promote hair growth and prevent dandruff.

Hibiscus tea is rich in antioxidants and helps in weight management and digestion.

🌍 Habitat

Hibiscus grows well in tropical and subtropical climates with plenty of sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Hibiscus is grown from cuttings and requires regular watering and well-drained soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption of hibiscus tea may lower blood pressure too much.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Anthocyanins: Antioxidant
Flavonoids: Anti-inflammatory
Vitamin C: Immunity booster
