🌿 Turmeric (Haldi)

The Golden Spice

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Turmeric / Haldi

Botanical Name: Curcuma longa

Family: Zingiberaceae

Origin: South Asia (India)

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Turmeric is widely known for its powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It is commonly used to boost immunity and improve overall health.

It helps in healing wounds, improving digestion, and reducing joint pain.

Turmeric milk (Haldi doodh) is a traditional remedy for cold and cough.

🌍 Habitat

Turmeric grows best in warm and humid climates with temperatures between 20°C to 30°C.

🌾 Cultivation

Turmeric is grown from rhizomes and requires well-drained fertile soil.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause stomach upset or allergic reactions in some individuals.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Curcumin: Anti-inflammatory
Turmerone: Brain health support
Zingiberene: Antioxidant
