🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Fennel (Saunf)

Botanical Name: Foeniculum vulgare

Family: Apiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🧬 Overview

Fennel (Foeniculum vulgare) is a medicinal herb known for its digestive, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Helps relieve bloating, gas, acidity, and supports digestion.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in temperate and tropical regions with sunny conditions.

🌾 Cultivation

Grown from seeds and needs moderate watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause sensitivity.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Anethole, Fenchone, Estragole
