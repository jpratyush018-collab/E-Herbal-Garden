🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Fennel (Saunf)

Botanical Name: Foeniculum vulgare

Family: Apiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Fennel is widely used to improve digestion and reduce bloating.

It helps relieve gas, acidity, and stomach cramps.

Fennel seeds are also known to freshen breath and support hormonal balance.

It has antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

Fennel grows best in sunny environments with well-drained soil.

It is commonly found in temperate and tropical regions.

🌾 Cultivation

Fennel is grown from seeds and requires full sunlight.

It needs moderate watering and grows well in loose, fertile soil.

The plant typically matures in 90–120 days.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause allergic reactions in some individuals.

Pregnant women should consult a doctor before consuming large amounts.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Anethole: Digestive aid
Fenchone: Antimicrobial
Estragole: Aromatic compound
