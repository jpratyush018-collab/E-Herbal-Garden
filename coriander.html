🌿 Coriander (Dhaniya)

The Digestive Herb

Home Plants About Contact

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Coriander / Dhaniya

Botanical Name: Coriandrum sativum

Family: Apiaceae

Origin: Mediterranean Region

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Coriander helps improve digestion and reduces bloating.

It supports blood sugar control and heart health.

It is also used for detoxification and boosting immunity.

🌍 Habitat

It grows in cool climates with well-drained soil and sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Coriander is grown from seeds and requires moderate watering.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause allergic reactions in some individuals.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Linalool: Digestive aid
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
Essential Oils: Antibacterial
