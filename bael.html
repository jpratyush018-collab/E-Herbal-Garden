🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Bael / Bilva

Botanical Name: Aegle marmelos

Family: Rutaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Bael is widely used for treating digestive disorders such as diarrhea and constipation.

It helps in detoxifying the body and improving gut health.

The leaves are also used in religious rituals and have medicinal value.

🌍 Habitat

Bael grows in dry and tropical regions and is highly drought-resistant.

🌾 Cultivation

It is grown from seeds and requires minimal care once established.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause constipation.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Marmelosin: Digestive aid
Tannins: Antimicrobial
Coumarins: Anti-inflammatory
