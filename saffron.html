🌿 Saffron (Kesar)

The Golden Spice of Kashmir

Home Plants About Contact

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Saffron / Kesar

Botanical Name: Crocus sativus

Family: Iridaceae

Origin: Mediterranean region (widely grown in Kashmir, India)

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Saffron is one of the most expensive spices in the world and is known for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

It helps improve mood, reduce stress, and is often used in treating depression.

Saffron is also beneficial for skin health, improving complexion and glow.

It supports heart health and improves digestion.

🌍 Habitat

Saffron grows best in cold regions with dry climates. It requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

🌾 Cultivation

Saffron is grown from corms and requires careful handling. It is mainly cultivated in Kashmir in India.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess consumption may cause dizziness, nausea, or allergic reactions.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Crocin: Gives color and antioxidant
Safranal: Aroma compound
Picrocrocin: Taste component
