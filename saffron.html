🧬 Overview

Saffron (Crocus sativus) is one of the most valuable medicinal and aromatic plants known for its antioxidant, mood-enhancing, and therapeutic properties.

The dried stigmas (threads) of the flower are the primary part used and contain powerful bioactive compounds.

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Saffron

Botanical Name: Crocus sativus

Family: Iridaceae

🌿 Medicinal Uses

  • Antioxidant protection
  • Improves mood and reduces stress
  • Supports heart health
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Supports brain function

🌍 Habitat

Grows in temperate regions with well-drained soil and sunny conditions.

🌾 Cultivation

  • Propagation through corms
  • Requires moderate watering
  • Needs sunny, dry conditions

⚠️ Precautions

  • Safe in moderate amounts
  • Excess may cause side effects

🧪 Key Compounds

  • Crocin
  • Safranal
  • Picrocrocin

💊 Applications

  • Mental wellness
  • Heart support
  • Cognitive health
