🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Dandelion

Botanical Name: Taraxacum officinale

Dandelion is a common flowering plant often considered a weed, but it is highly valued in herbal medicine for its nutritional and healing properties.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

🩺 Liver Health: Supports liver detoxification and function.

🍵 Digestive Aid: Helps improve digestion and appetite.

💧 Diuretic: Promotes urine production and reduces water retention.

🛡️ Antioxidant: Protects the body from oxidative stress.

🩸 Blood Purifier: Traditionally used to cleanse the blood.

🌍 Habitat

Dandelion grows worldwide in temperate regions. It thrives in grasslands, gardens, roadsides, and disturbed soils, making it a very adaptable plant.

🌾 Cultivation

Sunlight: Full sun to partial shade

Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil

Watering: Moderate watering

Propagation: Easily grown from seeds

Dandelion is hardy and grows easily with minimal care.

⚠️ Precautions

Generally safe when consumed in moderate amounts.

May cause allergic reactions in some individuals.

Excessive use may lead to stomach discomfort.

Consult a healthcare professional if pregnant or on medication.
