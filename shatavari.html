🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Shatavari

Botanical Name: Asparagus racemosus

Family: Asparagaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Shatavari is widely known for its role in supporting female reproductive health.

It helps in balancing hormones, improving fertility, and enhancing lactation.

It also acts as an adaptogen, helping the body cope with stress and boosting immunity.

🌍 Habitat

Shatavari grows in tropical and subtropical regions and prefers well-drained soil.

🌾 Cultivation

It is cultivated using roots and requires moderate watering and sunlight.

⚠️ Precautions

Should be used under guidance in case of hormonal conditions.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Saponins: Hormonal balance
Alkaloids: Anti-inflammatory
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
