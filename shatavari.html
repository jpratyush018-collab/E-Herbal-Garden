🧬 Overview

Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus) is a medicinal plant known for its adaptogenic and rejuvenating properties, especially for hormonal and reproductive health.

The roots are the primary part used.

🌱 Botanical & General Information

Family: Asparagaceae

Plant Type: Climbing plant with tuberous roots

Habitat: Tropical and subtropical regions

Parts Used: Roots and leaves

🌿 Propagation & Cultivation

Seeds: Common method, takes time to germinate

Root Division: Faster and more effective
