🌿 Black Pepper (Kali Mirch)

The King of Spices

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Black Pepper / Kali Mirch

Botanical Name: Piper nigrum

Family: Piperaceae

Origin: India

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Black pepper is known for improving digestion and boosting metabolism.

It helps in relieving cold, cough, and respiratory problems.

It also enhances nutrient absorption and has strong antioxidant properties.

🌍 Habitat

Black pepper grows in tropical regions with warm temperatures and high humidity.

🌾 Cultivation

It is cultivated from cuttings and requires support to climb like a vine.

⚠️ Precautions

Excess intake may cause irritation or stomach issues.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Piperine: Improves absorption
Essential Oils: Antimicrobial
Flavonoids: Antioxidant
