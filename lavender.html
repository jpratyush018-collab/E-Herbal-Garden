🧬 Overview

Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia) is a highly valued medicinal and aromatic herb known for its calming fragrance and therapeutic benefits. It has been used for centuries in traditional medicine, aromatherapy, and cosmetics.

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Lavender

Botanical Name: Lavandula angustifolia

Family: Lamiaceae

Plant Type: Perennial flowering shrub

🌿 Medicinal Uses

🧠 Stress Relief: Reduces anxiety and promotes relaxation

😴 Sleep Aid: Improves sleep quality and treats insomnia

🦠 Antimicrobial: Helps fight bacteria and infections

🔥 Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain

🧴 Skin Care: Helps in treating acne and skin irritation

🍵 Uses in Daily Life

🌿 Herbal teas for relaxation

🛏️ Used in pillows for better sleep

🧴 Essential oils in perfumes and cosmetics

🕯️ Used in candles and aromatherapy

🌿 Cultivation

Sunlight: Full sunlight

Soil: Well-drained sandy soil

Watering: Low to moderate

Climate: Warm and dry

🧪 Key Compounds

Linalool, Linalyl acetate, flavonoids, tannins, essential oils

💊 Health Benefits

✔ Reduces anxiety and depression

✔ Improves sleep quality

✔ Supports skin healing

✔ Boosts immunity

🏡 Home Remedies

🌸 Lavender Tea: Relieves stress and aids digestion

💆 Oil Massage: Reduces headaches and muscle pain

🌬️ Aromatherapy: Improves mood and relaxation

🌼 Interesting Facts

✨ Used in ancient Roman baths

🌿 Naturally repels insects

💜 Known as the “herb of calmness”

⚠️ Precautions

Use in moderate amounts

Essential oil should be diluted before use

May cause irritation in sensitive individuals
