🌿 Tulsi (Holy Basil)

The Queen of Medicinal Herbs

🌱 Basic Information

Common Name: Tulsi / Holy Basil

Botanical Name: Ocimum sanctum

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Indian Subcontinent

🌿 Medicinal Uses

Tulsi is a powerful medicinal herb used in Ayurveda. It boosts immunity, helps in cold, cough, asthma, and reduces stress. It also has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

🌍 Habitat

Grows in tropical climates like India. Needs sunlight and warm temperature.

🌾 Cultivation

Can be grown easily using seeds or stem cuttings.

⚠️ Precautions

Avoid excessive use. Consult doctor if needed.

🧪 Chemical Compounds

Eugenol: Anti-inflammatory
Ursolic Acid: Anti-cancer
Rosmarinic Acid: Antioxidant
